# Task 1: Brief Description
I chose Canva for my presentation due to its intuitive interface, which lets anyone produce polished designs without graphic design expertise. The project dives into digital literacy—our capacity to effectively locate, assess, and disseminate information securely via digital tools—highlighting how it merges tech proficiency with critical thinking for safe and clear online interactions.

Key Topics Covered
Slides address core internet safety practices, such as crafting robust passwords and enabling multi-factor authentication, alongside vital professional email norms like replying promptly within 24 hours. They also stress vigilance against suspicious links to foster responsible digital behavior.

Canva's Role

What stood out was Canva's templates, which transformed dense guidelines into sleek, scannable visuals—making complex advice on online respect and caution far more engaging and accessible.
