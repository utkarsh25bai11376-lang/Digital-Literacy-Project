# Online Safety Checklist

## Everyday Precautions

Create strong, unique passwords for every account.

Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever available.

Skip suspicious links and untrusted attachments.

Regularly update devices and apps for security fixes.

Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive tasks.

Confirm sender legitimacy before replying to emails, texts, or calls.

## UPI and Money Protection

Keep your UPI PIN, OTP, and bank info private—never share.

Ignore unknown QR codes or payment approvals from strangers.

Verify recipient details twice before any transfer.

Stick to verified, official payment apps.

## Report Incidents Fast

File cyber fraud reports at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Dial the 24/7 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
